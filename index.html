Who we are.

Coffee Cats is a company that combines the love of coffee and cats with the art of color arrangement. We offer a range of products and services that cater to coffee lovers, cat lovers, and color enthusiasts. Whether you are looking for a cozy coffee mug with a cute cat design, a colorful palette of coffee beans to create your own blends, or a professional color consultation for your home or office, we have something for you. Our mission is to bring joy and harmony to your life through coffee, cats, and color. Visit us online or at our store to see our latest collections and promotions. About us
Come meet the team!

We are a quirky bunch of enthusiasts. Learn more about us below!

Evelyn Bright, founder and CEO of Coffee Cats, She is a renowned color expert and a successful entrepreneur who has built a fortune from her innovative color products and services. She is also the creator of the Bright Color Wheel, a revolutionary tool that helps users create harmonious and meaningful color schemes based on their personality, mood, and goals. Evelyn has a passion for color and its effects on human perception, emotion, and behavior. She has studied color theory extensively and has a master’s degree in color science from the University of Cambridge. She has published several books and articles on color theory and has been featured in many media outlets as a color authority. Evelyn lives in a colorful mansion with her husband, three children, and seven cats, all of whom have names inspired by colors

Marie Westmoreland is a self-proclaimed crazy cat lady and a passionate photographer who specializes in capturing purrfect photos of feline friends. She has a collection of over 300 cats, most of them rescued from the streets or shelters, and she treats them like her own children. She loves photographing them as they relax, play, or pose around her home, which is filled with cat-themed decor and furniture. She also offers her services to other cat-lovers who want to immortalize their furry companions in stunning portraits. She believes that cats are mysterious and magical creatures that deserve to be celebrated and admired.

Dave MacDonnell is a lifelong coffee enthusiast and homebrewer who enjoys writing for coffee websites and sampling every kind of coffee known to man. He’s tried unusual coffees from all over the world and owns an unhealthy amount of coffee gear. He loves experimenting with different brewing methods, roasting his own beans, and discovering new coffee trends. He can’t start his day without a cup of freshly ground coffee and can’t end it without a shot of espresso. He believes that coffee is not just a drink, but a way of life.
